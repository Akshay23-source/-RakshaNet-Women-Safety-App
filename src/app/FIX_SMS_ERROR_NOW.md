## ⚡ QUICK FIX (Follow These Steps Exactly)

### Step 1: Open Twilio Console
**Click this link now** → https://console.twilio.com/us1/develop/phone-numbers/manage/verified

### Step 2: Click "Add New Caller ID"
Look for the red **"+ Add new Caller ID"** button on the page

### Step 3: Enter Your Phone Number
```
Format: +[country code][your number]

Examples:
India:     +919876543210
USA:       +14155551234
UK:        +447911123456
```

### Step 4: Click "Verify"
Twilio will call your phone **immediately**

### Step 5: Answer the Call
- Pick up your phone
- Listen to the automated message
- You'll hear a verification code
- Enter it when prompted

### Step 6: Confirm Success
You should see your number with a **green checkmark** ✅

### Step 7: Test RakshaNet Again
1. Open RakshaNet app
2. Enter the **SAME phone number** you just verified
